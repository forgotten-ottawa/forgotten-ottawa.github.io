January 16, 1905

Stanley Cup Kick
Final game of the Stanley Cup playoffs between the Ottawa Silver Sevens and the Dawson City Nuggets. The game took place at Dey's Arena in Ottawa with 2000 spectators in attendance. Ottawa won 23-2. Ottawa celebrates by kicking the Stanley Cup across the canal.
